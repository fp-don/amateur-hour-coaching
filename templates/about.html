Meet Your Coach

Alyssa - Founder & Head Coach

From Fargo, ND, Alyssa brings a lifetime of athletic experience combined with professional expertise to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Alyssa's journey in athletics began early with a passion for basketball, soccer, track, and cross-country. This love for sports led her to pursue a degree in Exercise Science at the University of Minnesota, where she earned her BS in Kinesiology.

Her passion for endurance sports emerged during her college years, leading her to complete numerous marathons and triathlons. As an Ironman finisher herself, Alyssa understands firsthand the dedication, training, and mental fortitude required to achieve ambitious athletic goals.

Balancing her roles as a coach, Ironman athlete, and mother, Alyssa deeply understands the challenges of integrating fitness into a busy life. She is dedicated to helping others achieve their fitness goals while maintaining balance with work, family, and personal commitments.
