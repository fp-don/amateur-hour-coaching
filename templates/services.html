Triathlon Coaching

Tailored training plans that fit your schedule and lifestyle, preparing you for optimal race performance.

Our triathlon coaching programs are designed to help athletes of all levels achieve their goals. Whether you're preparing for your first sprint triathlon or training for an Ironman, we create customized programs for swimming, biking, and running that accommodate your schedule and prepare you to perform at your best on race day.

  • Personalized training plans for swim, bike, and run
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your lifestyle

First-time triathletes, experienced athletes looking to improve times, anyone training for sprint, Olympic, half-Ironman, or Ironman distances.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What fitness level do I need to start?

Our programs are designed for all fitness levels, from complete beginners to experienced athletes. We'll tailor your plan based on your current fitness level and goals.

How much time do I need to commit?

Training time varies based on your goals and availability. We design programs that fit your schedule, whether you have 3 hours or 15 hours per week to dedicate to training.

Can I train if I have a busy work and family schedule?

Absolutely! As a working mother herself, Alyssa understands the challenges of balancing training with life's demands. Programs are specifically designed to accommodate busy schedules.

Do I need special equipment?

Equipment needs vary by program. We'll discuss your current equipment during consultation and provide recommendations. Many programs can start with minimal equipment.

How often will we communicate?

Communication frequency depends on your chosen program. This can range from weekly check-ins to email support as needed. We'll establish a schedule that works for you.

What if I get injured or need to take time off?

Life happens! We'll adjust your training plan as needed for injuries, illness, or other life events. The goal is sustainable progress, not pushing through at any cost.
